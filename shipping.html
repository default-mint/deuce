Shipping

Last updated: January 2026

At Topspin Tennis Supply, we aim to process and deliver your order as quickly and reliably as possible. Please review our shipping terms below.

Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1 business day after successful payment confirmation (excluding weekends and US public holidays). Once your order has been processed and dispatched, you will receive a shipment confirmation email with tracking.

Delivery Timeframes

We ship orders using trusted carrier partners, USPS, UPS, and FedEx. The shipping carrier is selected automatically based on your delivery location and operational availability.

Estimated delivery times after dispatch:

Region Estimated Delivery
Continental United States4-7 business days
Alaska & Hawaii7-10 business days
US Territories (PR, GU, etc.)10-14 business days

Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to carrier operations, public holidays, or circumstances beyond our control.

Shipping Fees

Topspin Tennis Supply offers free standard shipping on US orders over $100. Orders below $100 carry a flat $7.99 standard shipping fee. Expedited shipping options, where available, are displayed at checkout and apply only to the specific offer selected at the time of purchase. All applicable shipping costs are shown before payment is completed.

International Shipping

Topspin Tennis Supply ships within the United States only. We hope to open international shipping in the future, sign up at checkout for early access.

Failed or Delayed Deliveries

If your order has not been delivered within the estimated delivery timeframe, please contact our customer support team. If an order is confirmed as lost in transit or cannot be delivered, you may request a full refund in accordance with applicable consumer protection laws.

Incorrect Address Information

Customers are responsible for providing accurate and complete shipping details at checkout. We are not responsible for delivery delays or non-delivery caused by incorrect or incomplete address information. If a parcel is returned to us due to an undeliverable address, we will contact you to arrange re-shipment at the original shipping cost.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding shipping or delivery, please contact our customer support team at help@deuce.com.
